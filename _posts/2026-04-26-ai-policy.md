It should take only a couple of braincells to see that I've slopcoded most of the templating for this site.
You can see my whole GEMINI.md in the github repository, and my goal is for AI generated code to be transparent.

However, I do not use AI to generate any actual <i>content</i> on this site. 
The content is me typing manual keystrokes on my keyboard.
I might make an exception for code snippets if I feel a code snippet is warranted for a post.
If, in those cases, I use AI to generate the code, I will clearly label it as such. 

On the off chance of overwhelming evidence in the future that I should completely offload my thinking to the robots, 
I will either revisit this policy or go become a goat farmer.
